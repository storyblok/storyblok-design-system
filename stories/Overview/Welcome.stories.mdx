# About Blok.Ink

**_We will not actively maintain or update the Design System for the foreseeable future. The [code repository](https://github.com/storyblok/storyblok-design-system) is now archived. The [NPM package](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@storyblok/design-system) will remain public, but it will not receive updates during this period._**

Welcome to the Blok.Ink Design System!

Our design system is the backbone of the [app.storyblok.com](https://app.storyblok.com) app ensuring that every aspect of our user experience is consistent and beautiful.

To get started, check out [our tutorial](https://www.storyblok.com/docs/guide/in-depth/design-system) which provides an overview of how to use the design system and its components. You can also visit our [website](https://www.storyblok.com/) to learn more about Storyblok, access our documentation, and get in touch with the product documentations teams who are responsible for documenting the system.

We understand that issues can arise, which is why we encourage you to [report any problems](https://github.com/storyblok/storyblok-design-system/issues) you encounter with our design system. By working together, we can make sure that Blok.Ink continues to be an outstanding user experience for our customers.
